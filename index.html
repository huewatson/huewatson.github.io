Hi there ~

I'm a user experience researcher that strives to understand the complex relationships between humans and technology. I'm passionate about understanding users and representing their perspectives through research. And in turn, this helps to inform the development of technology that supports people and their behaviors.

In my day-to-day, I enjoy thinking about how technology affects society and how it is integrated into our daily lives. You can check out some of my work here.

Computer Vision & AR

I've worked on various projects with Google AR & VR and Google Lens, where I conducted user research to help inform early product decisions.

I've collaborated on over 30 projects using a variety of methods including concept testing, usability testing, diary studies, literature reviews, and more.
